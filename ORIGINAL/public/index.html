Welcome to the Cursed Mines

As you enter the mines the ground shakes violently and all around you huge chunks of rocks fall down. After a few seconds it is all over, but as the dust settles you find out that the entrance is completely blocked by huge boulders.

But all is not lost, for somewhere in this most foul place, the lost pages from the legendary Book of Knowledge are hidden.

Many adventurers tried to find them and gain the magical powers from their forgotten secrets... but they all failed and their dusty bones now litter the ground of the abandoned mines...

One of the pages will surely be powerful enough to crush the boulders and allow you to escape.
